💡 Key Market Gaps

❌ No All-in-One Platform

No single competitor combines event organizing + group payments + vendor marketplace. They all solve only 1 of 3 problems.

❌ No Event Escrow in SEA

Escrow.com exists for transactions but isn't event-friendly. No platform offers progressive milestone escrow for group events in Southeast Asia.

❌ Social UX vs Payments

Partiful has incredible social UX (9/10) but zero payment capability (1/10). Eventbrite has payments but terrible social features (4/10).

❌ No SEA Service Marketplace

GigSalad, Thumbtack, The Bash are US-only. Zaloha in SG is tiny. No credible event services marketplace exists for Singapore/SEA.

❌ PayNow Underutilized

PayNow is ubiquitous in Singapore but no event platform deeply integrates it. People still do awkward bank transfers for group events.

❌ Mobile-First is Rare

Most event management tools are desktop-first. Only Klook (9/10) and Partiful (9/10) have truly great mobile UX.

⚖️ SG Regulatory Considerations

Payment Services Act (PSA) 2019

MAS licensing required for handling payments. Escrow needs specific licensing.

PDPA

Strict data protection for attendee info collection, use, storage.

GST (9%)

Applies to event services. GST registration required if revenue >S$1M.

Public Entertainment License

Events with 150+ people or serving alcohol need SPF license.

AML/CFT Compliance

Group payment/escrow platforms must comply with anti-money laundering rules.

🎨 Design Rationale — What We Borrowed & Improved

✓ Ideas Borrowed

  • Partiful's fun, social-first event UX and playful tone
  • Luma's clean, minimal event page design
  • Klook's mobile-first approach and SEA localization
  • Peerspace's marketplace UX with add-on services
  • Escrow.com's milestone-based payment release model
  • Splitwise's intuitive group balance tracking
  • Honeybook's vendor management tools
  • Kickstarter's progress bar and social proof mechanics

🔥 Weaknesses We Improved

  • Eventbrite's terrible group payment support → built-in escrow
  • Partiful's zero payment capability → progressive milestones
  • Splitwise's tracking-only approach → actual collection + escrow
  • Sistic's dated, legacy technology → modern, vibrant design
  • GigSalad's US-only limitation → SEA-first marketplace
  • Meetup's clunky, outdated UX → mobile-first, beautiful interface
  • EventNook's corporate-only focus → social events for friends
  • WhatsApp group chaos → structured event + payment flow
